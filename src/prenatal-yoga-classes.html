There are 3 pillars of Garbh Wisdom Holistic Yoga Program

1) Garbh Samskar led Dincharya

  • Garbh Samwad - A pdf is shared on tips of Fetal Talk for the new moms.
  • Garbh Aahar - Trimester specific Pregnancy Diet is shared after reviewing the current diet and any medical condition.
  • Garbh Naad - Group Mantra chanting sessions are conducted weekly.
  • Garbhini Paricharya - A pdf is shared on Prenatal Self Care Tips.
  • Garbh Vichar - Scientific tools such as affirmations and visualization are practiced to train the subconscious mind and break the old patterns. A pdf is shared for the weekly affirmations and visualisations are practiced after every asanas class.
  • Garbh Vyavhar - Dincharya is discussed as per your current lifestyle, tips shared.

2) Garbh Yogabhyasa

  • Trimester Specific Prenatal Hatha Yoga Asanas
  • Pranayama Practise (includes labour and birthing breathwork)
  • Garbh Dhyana (Meditation upon baby)
  • Aum Chanting
  • Labour Drills post 34weeks
  • Garbh Vyavhar - Dincharya is discussed as per your current lifestyle, tips shared

3) Labour & Childbirth Education

From Stages of Labour to Birth Preferences to How to spend the Delivery Day to Birthing Techniques to Golden Hour to First Feed (Colostrum) and many more. Emotional support in the 9th month . Pooja ji will be available on call personally to monitor the labour.
