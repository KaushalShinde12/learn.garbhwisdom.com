Why Join This Workshop?

Labor can feel overwhelming — but it doesn’t have to be. When you and your partner know what to expect, how to cope, and how to work together, birth transforms into an empowering experience.

In this power-packed session, you’ll learn:

  • Early signs and Stages of labor and what’s normal.
  • Pain relief & coping techniques for Labor: Breathing, Movement, Massage, and Position.
  • Partner’s role in reducing pain, boosting confidence, and managing labor at home.
  • How to communicate effectively during contractions and hospital interactions.
  • Understanding medical interventions & how to make informed choices.

Who is this for?

  • Expecting mothers in their 3rd trimester
  • Partners who want to be hands-on, confident birth supporters.
  • Couples who want clarity on hospital process, birth options, and pain management

What You’ll Walk Away With

  • Clear understanding of the labor process
  • Confidence to handle contractions at home and in hospital
  • Pain relief techniques that really work
  • An informed, educated and reliable Partner
  • Practical tips for the Golden Hour
  • Printable Birth Plan Template

Who is a Birth Partner?

Your birth partner is anyone you choose to be by your side during labour and birth to support you-emotionally, physically, and mentally.

They could be:

  • Your husband/partner
  • Mother or sister
  • Close friend
  • Doula
  • Mother-in-law or another trusted family member

Their role is to comfort, encourage, and help you follow your birth plan.
They don’t need medical knowledge-just a caring heart and willingness to support you.

You can attend my Labour & Childbirth Education Workshop with your birth partner-whoever that may be. 💗

Know Your Host

Namaste, I’m Pooja V Nair, founder of Garbh Wisdom Academy.

I work with expecting and new mothers as a Prenatal & Postnatal Yoga Teacher and Childbirth Educator, blending modern evidence-based knowledge with the timeless wisdom of Garbh Sanskar.

Over the years, I’ve seen one thing very clearly — a well-prepared birth partner changes everything. A calm, confident supporter can turn fear into courage, pain into manageable effort, and uncertainty into trust.

That’s why I created this Partner Workshop — to give you and your chosen birth companion the tools, confidence, and connection you need for a positive birth experience.

What I bring to this workshop

  • Practical wisdom from working closely with expecting moms and their families
  • Evidence-based childbirth education so you feel informed, not fearful
  • Traditional Garbh Sanskar practices blended with modern birthing techniques
  • A safe, non-judgmental space for you and your birth partner to ask, learn, and practice

My mission is simple
